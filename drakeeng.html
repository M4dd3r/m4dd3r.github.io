Drake (Aubrey Drake Graham) is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, music producer, and entrepreneur. He began his career as an actor on the series Degrassi: The Next Generation and started his music career in 2006. After signing with Young Money Entertainment in 2009, Drake rose to prominence with the EP So Far Gone (2009), which featured the hit "Best I Ever Had". His debut album, Thank Me Later (2010), and second album, Take Care (2011), solidified his fame, with Take Care winning the Grammy for "Best Rap Album". Drake continued releasing successful albums, including Nothing Was the Same (2013), Views (2016), and More Life (2017). In 2018, he released Scary Hours, followed by his fifth album, Scorpion, which featured hits like "God's Plan". His sixth album, Certified Lover Boy (2021), broke records, and he continued releasing hits with Honestly, Nevermind (2022) and Her Loss (2022), his collaborative album with 21 Savage. In 2023, he released For All the Dogs, featuring new hits. With over 170 million songs sold in the U.S., Drake is one of the most successful global music artists.
